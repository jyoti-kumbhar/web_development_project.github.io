Our Services

At Mehndi Designer, we provide a wide range of mehndi styles to suit every occasion and preference. Whether you’re preparing for a wedding, festival, or personal event, our services are tailored to enhance your experience with beautiful, intricate designs.

Discover the magic of handcrafted mehndi and let me adorn your celebrations with the beauty and elegance of henna art.

  • 1. Arabic Mehndi Design 
    
                Characterized by bold lines and flowing patterns, Arabic mehndi designs are a perfect choice for those who prefer a minimal yet striking look. The large motifs often leave spaces between them, giving the design an elegant feel.
  • 2. Bridal Mehndi Design 
    
                For brides, our intricate and detailed bridal mehndi designs are crafted to tell your unique story. Covering the hands, arms, and feet, these designs are filled with delicate patterns, motifs, and personal elements to make your special day even more memorable.
  • 3. Indian Mehndi Design 
    
                Traditional and timeless, Indian mehndi designs are renowned for their fine lines, detailed motifs, and intricate patterns. These designs often cover large areas and feature elements like peacocks, paisleys, and floral designs.
  • 4. Portrait Mehndi Design
                A contemporary and artistic service, portrait mehndi involves creating detailed portraits or figures within the mehndi design. Ideal for adding a personal touch, whether it's a bride and groom or a favorite deity.
  • 5. Moroccan Mehndi Design 
    
                For a unique and geometric aesthetic, Moroccan mehndi designs focus on symmetrical shapes, angular patterns, and bold lines. This style is perfect for those who prefer non-traditional, edgy looks
  • 6. Jewellery Mehndi Design 
    
                Inspired by the patterns of traditional jewelry, this design style mimics necklaces, bangles, rings, and other adornments. It’s perfect for brides and special events, giving the illusion of wearing intricate henna-crafted jewelry. 
  • 7. Minimalist Mehndi Design 
    
                For those who prefer simplicity, minimalist mehndi designs focus on delicate, fine lines and smaller motifs. This style is perfect for those looking for something subtle yet beautiful
