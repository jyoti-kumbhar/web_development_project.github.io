Book Your Mehndi Appointment

We are thrilled to be part of your special day! To book a mehndi design session, please follow the steps below:

How to Book:

  • Check our Services page to choose a design that suits your needs.
  • Visit our Gallery for inspiration and design ideas.

Booking Details:

Please note that booking requests should be made at least 2 weeks in advance for bridal designs and 1 week for regular sessions. A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment.

Payment Information:

We accept online payments, bank transfers, and cash on the day of the appointment. Payment details will be provided once your booking is confirmed.

Contact Us:

For any questions or to discuss custom designs, feel free to reach out to us at email: mehndidesigner108@gmail.com or call us at 7021499130.
